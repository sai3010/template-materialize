About Us

D K Chhajer & Co., is 50 years old Chartered Accountants (CA) Firm. The firm is managed by thirteen dynamic and vibrant CAs as partners based in seven different cities in India - at Kolkata (HO), Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi, Port Blair, Bhubaneswar & Hyderabad.

The firm provides services & consultancy relating to accounting & implementation thereof including IFRS (International Financial Reporting Standards), auditing including Internal, Statutory, Tax, concurrent and management audits, matters relating to taxation including International Taxation & Transfer Pricing (TP), Outsourcing of accounts, payroll, stores, finance, payable / receivable management, statutory compliances, SEBI laws, FEMA, companies law matters, etc.
